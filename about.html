About Project Allocation

This work will discuss the design and implementation of online Students Project Allocation System, a software system that tends to provide an alternative solution to the usual students project allocation system of Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas Bonny, Rivers State, Nigeria. The case Study is Computer Science Department. Using this system, the admin user is expected to create an account and invite the students to join the system. After this is successfully done, they can login to propose for projects. Each student is expected to submit three proposals to their supervisor or choose from the available project topics created by the admin.

Benefits

  • Solve the problem of using a manual system (pen and paper registering) in the management and allocation of projects.
  • Eliminates duplication in project topics approved for students.
  • Promotes proper documentation of allocated project topics.
  • Efficiency in entering, updating and retrieving records of allocated projects.
  • Easy accessing of project supervisors for approval of topic.
